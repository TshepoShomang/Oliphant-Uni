Contact Us - Submit Your Application

If you're ready to submit your application, please follow the instructions below:

Instructions for Submitting Your Application:

  1. Prepare your application document (including personal details, academic information, and any supporting documents).
  2. Write a brief cover letter (if applicable) explaining why you're interested in applying and what you're seeking from the program.
  3. Send the completed application to our email: contact@universityservices.com
  4. Ensure you include the following information in the subject line of your email: "Application for University Services for - Your Full Name"
  5. After submitting, wait for a confirmation email from us within 2-3 business days.
