Meet Our Director

Mr. Zolile Oliphant Beauchamp

Mr. Zolile Oliphant Beauchamp is the visionary founder and director of our University Application Services company. With a passion for education and years of experience in academic support, he has helped countless students gain admission into top institutions across the country.

His mission is to bridge the gap between learners and their dream universities by providing personalised, affordable, and results-driven support throughout the application process. Whether you're in Grade 12, taking a gap year, or upgrading, Mr. Beauchamp is committed to seeing you succeed.
